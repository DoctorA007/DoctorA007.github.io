📋 Essential Formulas

Quadratic Formula

x = (-b ± √(b² - 4ac)) / 2a

Used to find the roots of quadratic equations of the form ax² + bx + c = 0

Newton's Second Law

F = ma

Force equals mass times acceleration

Pythagorean Theorem

a² + b² = c²

In a right triangle, the square of the hypotenuse equals the sum of squares of the other two sides

Area of Circle

A = πr²

Area equals pi times radius squared

Kinematic Equation

v = u + at

Final velocity = initial velocity + acceleration × time

Energy Conservation

KE + PE = constant

Kinetic Energy + Potential Energy remains constant in isolated systems
